TikTok's latest indulgence is... reading?

We made reading social with the first-ever collective e-reader — a platform where users can highlight, react, and comment on every page. All to launch TikTok Books from BookTok, turning passive scrolling into shared literary experiences.

Art Direction
Concept Creation
Product / UX
INSIGHT
BookTok proved Gen-Z reads — but the reading experience itself hasn't evolved since Kindle. Books are still a solitary medium in a social-first generation.
STRATEGY
Position TikTok as the platform that makes reading communal — leveraging BookTok's organic momentum to launch a product that feels native to how Gen-Z already consumes content.
IDEA
A social-first e-reader where every page is a feed. Highlight a passage, and your followers see it. React to a plot twist in real time. Reading becomes performing.
FORMAT
Swipe to turn pages. Tap to react. Comment on paragraphs like threads. The book becomes the content — and the community becomes the algorithm.
