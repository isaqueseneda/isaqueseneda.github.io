chaos
Heineken wanted to overturn staff shortage in the hospitality industry. So our strategy followed 3 premises:
(1) We found out that Gen-Z don't want to work in bars due to lack of future prospects;
(2) But working in bars gives you lots of soft skills (teamwork, etc.);
(3) So we reframed bar experience as a skill badge on Linkedin.

Bronze in Direct: Food & Drink. Shortlist in (1) Social Insights & Engagement: Audience Targeting / Engagement Strategies, (2) Creative B2B: Targeting & Engagement, (3) Creative B2B: Cross-Channel Storytelling, (4) Creative Business Transformation [Business Design & Operations]: Company Culture. Badge mechanic ideated with Alejandro Gutierrez and Nikolay Malchev.
