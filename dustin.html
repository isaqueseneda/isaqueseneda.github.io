Meet Dustin — a tiny dust bunny on the run from the unstoppable Vorwerk vacuum cleaner.

A TV commercial concept that follows his relentless escape through the house, turning a mundane cleaning task into a cinematic chase sequence. Because even dust deserves a dramatic exit.

Art Direction
Concept Creation
TV / Film
INSIGHT
People have an emotional bond with the chaos of their homes — even with the dust. Cleaning feels like an act of destruction, not care.
STRATEGY
Reframe Vorwerk's power as inevitable — not aggressive. Make the product the unstoppable force in a story where even the dust tries to survive.
IDEA
Give dust a face, a name, and a will to live — then watch Vorwerk catch up anyway.
TONE
Pixar-meets-horror. Empathy for the villain. A chase film where you root for the wrong side.
